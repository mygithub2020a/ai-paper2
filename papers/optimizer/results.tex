\subsection{Ablation Studies}

We systematically ablate components to understand their contributions:

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{No damping} ($\damping = 0$): Reduces to noisy gradient descent
\item \textbf{No exploration} ($\exploration = 0$): Deterministic with quadratic damping
\item \textbf{Additive noise}: Replace $\exploration \cdot g \cdot \epsilon$ with $\exploration \cdot \epsilon$
\item \textbf{Fixed parameters}: Disable adaptation mechanisms
\end{itemize}
